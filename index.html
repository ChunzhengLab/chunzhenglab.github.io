About Me

Hello! I am Chunzheng Wang, a PhD student at Fudan University. Currently, I am visiting the Organisation Européenne pour la Recherche Nucléaire (CERN) for a two-year period. My research focuses on the upgrade of the ALICE experiment's ITS3 detector and theoretical and phenomenological studies of chiral anomaly effects in relativistic heavy-ion collisions.
