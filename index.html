About Me

Hello! I am Chunzheng Wang, a PhD student at Institute of Modern Physics, Fudan University (Supervisor: Prof. Ma Yugang, Prof. Shou Qiye, Prof. Zhang Song). Currently, I am visiting the European Organization for Nuclear Research (CERN) for a two-year period (Supervisor: Fabrizio Grosa). My research focuses on the upgrade of the ALICE ITS3 detector and phenomenological studies of chiral anomaly effects in relativistic heavy-ion collisions.
